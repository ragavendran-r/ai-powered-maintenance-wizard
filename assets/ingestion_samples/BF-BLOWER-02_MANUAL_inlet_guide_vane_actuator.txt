Inlet Guide Vane Actuator Maintenance Manual

Asset: BF-BLOWER-02
Equipment: Blast Furnace Combustion Air Blower
Area: Blast Furnace
Source type: manual

The inlet guide vane actuator controls blower outlet pressure during furnace ramp-up and steady combustion air operation. Pressure variance above 10 percent may indicate sticky actuator linkage, calibration drift, filter restriction, or unstable surge margin.

Maintenance checks:

- Verify actuator stroke feedback from 0 percent to 100 percent command.
- Inspect linkage pins, bushings, and clevis fasteners for wear or binding.
- Clean actuator linkage and apply approved high-temperature lubricant.
- Validate pressure transmitter calibration before replacing actuator hardware.
- Confirm furnace supervisor notification when high pressure variance persists for two samples.

Safety:

Use the blower isolation permit before hands-on actuator inspection. Keep personnel clear of linkage motion while testing actuator response.

Recommended spares:

Actuator linkage kit, position feedback sensor, inlet filter element, pressure transmitter calibration kit.
