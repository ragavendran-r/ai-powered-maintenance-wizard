# Main Drive Bearing Vibration Inspection SOP

Asset: RM-DRIVE-01
Equipment: Hot Strip Mill Main Drive Motor
Area: Hot Rolling Mill
Source type: sop

## Trigger Conditions

Start this SOP when drive end vibration rises above 7.1 mm/s or increases by more than 2.0 mm/s above the recent operating baseline for two consecutive samples.

## Safety Controls

Apply lockout and tagout before removing motor or coupling guards. Confirm the mill drive is isolated, stored energy is released, and the inspection permit is approved by the shift maintenance lead.

## Inspection Steps

1. Confirm the vibration reading against the historian and handheld vibration meter.
2. Inspect drive end spherical roller bearing temperature and lubrication condition.
3. Check coupling alignment, coupling bolt tightness, and foundation bolt torque marks.
4. Inspect bearing housing looseness, soft foot indicators, and visible lubricant leakage.
5. If vibration remains above threshold after inspection, reduce mill load and schedule a controlled bearing inspection window.

## Required Spares

Drive end spherical roller bearing, coupling bolts, lubricant filter, temperature sensor, and bearing housing gasket set.
